Why Epicodus?

I wanted to go to Epicodus because I wanted to join the burgeoning tech industry. There are limitless possiblities in the field and that's very exciting to me. I like the pair programming format of Epicodus in particular because it allows for me to further develop my team-building and communication skills and work-ethic in addition to giving me a solid foundation in tech knowledge.
